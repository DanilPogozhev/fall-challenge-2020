<h3>cast / opp casts / learns / brews </h3>
* 0: recipeId: -1 - my bag, -2 - opponent's bag, >0 all other receipts, casts, brews.<br>                    
* 1: castType: 0 - cast, 1 - opp-cast, 2 - learn, 3 - brew<br>
* 2: first ingredient amount<br>
* 3: second ingredient amount<br>
* 4: third ingredient amount<br>
* 5: fourth ingredient amount<br>
* 6: price<br>
* 7: tomIndex - the amount of taxed tier-0 ingredients you gain from learning this spell<br>
* 8: taxCount - the amount of taxed tier-0 ingredients you gain from learning this spell<br>
* 9: re-castable: 1 - true, 0 - false<br>
* 10: repeatable: 1 - true, 0 - false.<br>

<h3>my bag / opponent's bag:</h3> 
* 0: first ingredient amount<br>
* 1: second ingredient amount<br>
* 2: third ingredient amount<br>
* 3: fourth ingredient amount<br>
<br>
* Current points.
